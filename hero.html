Tribute to Nelson Mandela

About

Nelson Mandela (1918 – 2013) was a South African leader who fought against apartheid. He became the first black president of South Africa in 1994 and is remembered for his courage, forgiveness, and leadership.

Achievements

  • Fought against apartheid
  • Spent 27 years in prison
  • Became South Africa’s first black president
  • Won the Nobel Peace Prize in 1993

Legacy

Mandela is remembered worldwide as a symbol of peace, unity, and justice.
